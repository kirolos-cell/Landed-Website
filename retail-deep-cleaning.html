Retail & Commercial Cleaning

Retail deep cleaning
for Melbourne

Your team keeps the place tidy day to day. We do the deep clean they cannot: above the lights, behind the shelving, and every spot a daily wipe-down never reaches. For shops, showrooms, gyms, clinics and offices.

What It Is

The clean your
staff cannot do

Most teams keep their floor presentable between customers. What they cannot do is get above the light fittings, pull out the shelving, or machine scrub the floor after close. That build-up is what customers notice, and it is exactly what we handle on a set schedule. We work across shopping centre tenancies, standalone shopfronts, showrooms, gyms, clinics and offices.

What Is Included

Every spot, properly

A full deep clean of your site, front of house to back of house. Scope is tailored to your space, but this is what a standard programme covers.

Above and out of reach

  • Light fittings, track lighting and above-light ledges
  • Air-conditioning vents, grilles and diffusers
  • Ceiling tiles, bulkheads and cornices
  • Sprinkler heads and smoke detectors
  • Exposed pipes, conduits and cable trays
  • High signage and light boxes

Behind and underneath

  • Behind and under shelving, racks and gondolas
  • Under counters, POS and till stations
  • Behind display cabinets and mirrors
  • Stockroom shelving and back walls
  • Skirting boards, edges and corners
  • Behind doors, hinges and door tracks

Glass, mirrors and display

  • Display cabinets inside and out
  • Shopfront glass and entry doors
  • Internal glass and partitions
  • Full-height and fitting room mirrors
  • Window tracks, sills and channels

Floors

  • Machine scrubbing of hard floors
  • Vinyl strip, seal and polish
  • Carpet steam clean and hot water extraction
  • Edges and corners machines miss
  • Entry mats and thresholds

Fixtures and fit-out

  • Display units, racks and mannequins
  • Counters and benchtops
  • Chrome, brass and metal polishing
  • Timber and joinery detail
  • Handrails and high-touch points

Back of house

  • Stockrooms and storage
  • Staff rooms and kitchenettes
  • Staff amenities deep clean and sanitise
  • Fridges, microwaves and sinks
  • Bin and waste areas

Specialist add-ons

  • Touchpoint sanitising and disinfection
  • Odour treatment
  • Upholstery and seating
  • Shopfront pressure washing
  • Post-fitout and post-renovation cleans
  • Peak season presentation cleans
  • End of lease and handover cleans
Your Programme

Set a schedule
that suits your site

Deep cleans work best on a rhythm. We set a frequency around your foot traffic, floor size and fit-out, then keep to it so the place never falls behind.

Fortnightly

High-traffic stores, gyms, food-adjacent tenancies and large floorplates where build-up happens fast and presentation cannot slip.

Most common Monthly

The right balance for most clothing and general retail, showrooms and offices. Steady foot traffic, a full reset every month, no surprises.

Quarterly

Smaller boutiques, jewellery, clinics and low-footfall showrooms that need a periodic deep reset rather than constant attention.

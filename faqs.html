Building Management & Strata Cleaning

A building manager looks after the day-to-day operation of a property so residents, tenants and committees do not have to. That covers coordinating maintenance and repairs, overseeing contractors and vendors, keeping common areas safe and well presented, staying on top of compliance, and responding when something goes wrong. Our dedicated building managers bring 9+ years of combined, hands-on experience across Melbourne buildings. In short, we keep the building running smoothly and protect its long-term value.
Strata or owners corporation management covers the administrative and financial side: levies, meetings, insurance, by-laws and the corporation’s legal obligations. Building management is the hands-on, on-the-ground side: maintenance, cleaning, contractors and the physical upkeep of the building. We focus on building management and work alongside your strata manager and committee, not in place of them.
We work with owners corporations, strata managers, building committees and private property owners across Melbourne, from individual residential and commercial buildings to larger property portfolios.

Retail Deep Cleaning

It is the periodic heavy clean that day to day tidying cannot cover. We work above the light fittings, behind and under shelving and displays, across glass and mirrors, floors, fixtures and back of house, so the parts of your space that quietly build up grime get properly reset.
Your team or your daily cleaner keeps the floor presentable between customers. A deep clean is the scheduled reset on top of that: high level work, machine scrubbing, and the spots that need equipment, time and access outside trading hours. We are not replacing your day to day cleaning, we are doing the part it cannot reach.
Most sites land on monthly. Fortnightly suits high traffic stores, gyms and large floorplates where build-up is fast. Quarterly suits smaller boutiques, jewellery and low footfall showrooms. We set the frequency after seeing the space.
Always. We clean early mornings, late nights or overnight so a deep clean never costs you a sale or gets in a customer's way.
Yes. We are inducted and insured, and we work to centre requirements for access, timing and paperwork so you are not chasing approvals on our behalf.
